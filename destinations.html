Destinations

Canaries

Our 7 day Canary Islands tour includes visits to Cueva del Viento lava tube and Teide National Park World Heritage Site on Tenerfie, Garajonay National Park World Heritage Site on La Gomera, Caldera de Taburiente National Park on La Palma and Timanfaya National Park on Lanzarote

Ecuador

Our 10 day Ecuador tour visits the active crater lake of Cuicocha, the ‘Avenue of the Volcanoes’ including Cotopaxi National Park and Rumiñahui, Sincholagua, Pasachoa and Antisana volcanoes, the dazzling emerald-green Laguna Quilotoa and ‘The Route of the Waterfalls’

Indonesia

Our 12 day Indonesian tour begins in Eaast Java with Krakatau, Papandayan and Galunggung volcanoes. Then in Central Java, Merapi volcano is our destination. Finally we move on to East Java where we encounter the Tengger caldera and Bromo, Semeru and Ijen volcanoes
