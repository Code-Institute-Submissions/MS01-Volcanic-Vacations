Destinations

Canaries

Our 7 day Canary Islands tour includes visits to Cueva del Viento lava tube and Teide National Park World Heritage Site on Tenerfie, Garajonay National Park World Heritage Site on La Gomera, Caldera de Taburiente National Park on La Palma and Timanfaya National Park on Lanzarote

Iceland

On Our 7 day tour to ‘The Land of Fire and Ice’ we visit Reykjanes Geopark, ‘The Golden Circle’ of Geysir, Gullfoss waterfall and Thingvellir National Park, the island of Heimaey, Seljalandsfoss waterfall, Vatnajokull National Park, Skogafoss waterfall, Jokulsarlon Lagoon, Dettifoss waterfall and Krafla

Italy

Our 7 day tour to Italy features all 5 active Italian volcanoes. Vulcano and Stromboli are located in the Aeolian Islands. We then visit Europe’s highest volcano, Sicily’s Mount Etna. In the Naples region we visit Mount Vesuvius and Campei Flegrei. We also visit Pompeii and Herculaneum World Heritage Sites

Ecuador

Our 10 day Ecuador tour visits locations such as the active volcanic crater lake of Cuicocha, the ‘Avenue of the Volcanoes’ including Cotopaxi National Park and the surrounding volcanoes, Rumiñahui, Sincholagua, Pasachoa and Antisana, the dazzling emerald-green Laguna Quilotoa and ‘The Route of the Waterfalls’

Hawaii

On our 10 day Hawaii tour we visit the Volcanoes National Park where we explore the Thurston Lava Tube and follow the ‘Crater Rim Drive’ around the Kilauea caldera, Rainbow Falls and Akaka Falls in the tropical rainforest region and also the astronomical observatories on the summit of Mauna Kea

US Pacific NW

Our 10 day tour of the Cascades range volcanoes of Washington and Oregon states visits Mount Rainier National Park with views of the Emmons glacier, Mount St Helens and Spirit Lake, Mount Hood, the spectacular Columbia River gorge and the Multnoma Falls, the 4th highest waterfall in the USA

Indonesia

Our 12 day Indonesian tour begins in West Java where we explore Krakatau, Papandayan and Galunggung volcanoes. Then in Central Java, Merapi volcano is our destination. Finally we move on to East Java where we encounter the Tengger caldera and Bromo, Semeru and Ijen volcanoes

Japan

Our 12 day tour to the ‘Land of the Rising Sun’ starts on the northern island of Hokkaido where we visit Akan National Park and Shikotsu-Tōya National Park. On Honshu we visit Towada-Hachimantai National Park, Mount Fuji and Mount Asama. On Kyushu we visit Mount Aso, Sakurajima and Tsurumi volcanoes

New Zealand

Our 12 day trip ‘Down Under’ begins on the North Island where we visit Rotorua and Taupo volcanic zones, White Island, Tongariro National Park, Mount Taranaki, Mount Ruapehu, Mount Tarawera and Mount Ngauruhoe. On the South Island we visit Fiordland, the Southern Alps and Mount Cook
